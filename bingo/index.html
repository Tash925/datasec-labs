Why I created this series

I created Career Roadmap Bingo as the kind of resource I wished I had when I began exploring cybersecurity. Career transitions can feel overwhelming when every roadmap presents a long list of tools, certifications, and job titles. These boards break the journey into smaller actions so learners can build confidence, recognize their progress, and keep moving forward. This is not a fixed curriculum—it is a living map that will continue to grow and change as I learn, build, and climb it myself.
